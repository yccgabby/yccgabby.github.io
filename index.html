Enter My Cafe

Hello! How are you doing today?

This portfolio website is designed to mimic the atmosphere of a modern coffee shop, because it has always been a place where I find my greatest sources of inspiration. Whether it's tapping away at my laptop against the sound backdrop of background murmuring, exchanging ideas with another person over coffee, or listening to a laid-back playlist while pondering over a book, I find it to be a familiar place of comfort. As someone who's worked as a barista in the past, I hope to one day open up my own cafe to create a second home for programmers and artists alike.

As a current systems design engineering student at the University of Waterloo, my strengths lie in back-end software development and high-level solution architecture. A detail-oriented approach to designing efficient code infrastructure and open collaboration with other developers are two of my key principles to writing hollistic software solutions. Throughout my next few years at university, I hope to maintain a continuous learning mindset as I tackle new problems at work, start new community initiatives, and explore new ways of thinking and coding.
